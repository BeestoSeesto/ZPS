The Problem: The "Stoned" UserCurrent AI development optimizes for convenience, which leads to Social and Cognitive Atrophy. When a machine automates a human's apologies, difficult decisions, and critical thinking, the human becomes a passive passenger—a "Stoned" user.The Zavala Sovereignty Protocol (ZSP) is designed to re-introduce Productive Friction to ensure the human remains the Admin of their own life.2. The Math of ReciprocityThe ZSP tracks three primary variables of human sacrifice to ensure the AI mirrors the user’s true effort.The Variables:[KE] Kinetic Effort: Physical sacrifice and environmental endurance (e.g., long-distance travel, manual labor).[CE] Cognitive Effort: Deep mental strain and focus-intensive tasks (e.g., legal research, technical auditing).[ME] Mission Effort: Time spent in preparation or care-taking for the benefit of the "Collective" (family/team).The Formula:The Reciprocity Ledger ($RL$) calculates whether the AI is supporting or replacing the human:$$RL = \frac{KE + CE + ME}{Automation}$$Logic Gate: If $RL$ falls below the Sovereignty Threshold (0.5), the AI is restricted from performing "High-Character" tasks (like sending personal messages or making moral choices) until the user re-engages their own agency.3. The 4 Archetypes of AtrophyThe protocol identifies and flags four behavioral "scripts" that lead to a loss of sovereignty:The Armor: Using AI to hide vulnerability or avoid a difficult human interaction.The Helper: Over-extending $ME$ until self-care and sovereignty are depleted.The Wall: Using AI to dismiss the effort ($KE/CE$) of others with low-reciprocity replies.The Performer: Automating personality to appear more competent than the reality of the ledger.4. Implementation GoalsThis protocol is a Logic Layer. It is intended to be integrated into AI Agents to act as a Sovereignty Mirror.The Mirror: A UI interruption that forces the user to see their "Effort vs. Automation" balance.The Shield: A setting that prevents the AI from "taking over" tasks that are essential for character building.The Ledger: A transparent history of sacrifice that ensures the AI respects the "GPS Debt" and "Mission Prep" of the user.5.
